Only type it once

Welcome to my website! Macrion is a project which allows you to turn your keyboard into a soundboard-like device that will simulate a series of key presses when you press just one. It is intended to make things easier when you are expected to do repetitive work, be it in excel, coding, or writing a paper where you type someone's name a million times, but never need to press one of your other keys. We hope you will give Macrion a try!
